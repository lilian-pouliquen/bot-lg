# Command soleil

## Details

<!-- --8<-- [start:details] -->
Description: Mute or unmute players.

Required role: `Game master`

Sub-commands:

* `se_leve`: Sun rises: players can debate
* `se_couche`: Sun sets: players are muted
<!-- --8<-- [end:details] -->

## Usage

<!-- --8<-- [start:usage] -->
Mute players:

```text
/soleil se_couche
```

Unmute players:

```text
/soleil se_leve
```
<!-- --8<-- [end:usage] -->
